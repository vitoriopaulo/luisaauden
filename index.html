Dear site visitor: Welcome to Luisa Auden's Jewellry Designer official website. Here you'll see a little bit of her path in the Jewellry Design field, Awards, Titles, Exhibitions, as well as selected Jewellry available for sale in the Store.

* Mission: Design functional, aesthetic pleasant and joyful Jellwery that, by mixing traditional hand-made techniques with cutting-edge manufacturing processess (i.e. 3D printing) disrupts the Status-Quo.

* Vision: Jewellry is more then a body accessory: it's Art, Design, Culture, Fashion, and mostly important - a way of self-expression.

* Values: Creativity, Irreverence, Boldness, Originality, Iconoclasm.
