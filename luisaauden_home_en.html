Luisa Auden is an American Jewelry Designer, born in May 2nd 2015 in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA. Very earlier in her childhood, it was clear her passion and incredible talent to the Visual Arts as a whole. Differently from the absolutely majority of her friends from highschool, she decided to not go to gollege. Instead, Luisa Auden built her path in the Jewelry Design industry in a very unique style: conducting independent research, traveling world-wide in order to learn from different cultures, as well as working as a sole-practitioner.

Her very unique, personal, criative, irreverent, out-of-the-box approach and understanding of Art and Design, resulted in a first Jewelry collection that totally disrupted the Status-Quo. In her earlier 20's, Luisa Auden won the majority of the most important Jewelry Design Awards not only in The United States, but World Wide. Her first creation, which make her became a star of the Design field of her generation, entitled - Lonely Ruby: a necklace she designed in a shape of two hearts (one smaller and other bigger, both coupled togheter) - in a tribute to her father Vitorio Paulo, a Brazilian Architect and an United States Immigrant - became a permanent piece of the Museum of Modern Art of New York (MOMA).

Currently in her earlier 30's Luisa Auden, after more then a decade of practice in the Jewelry Design industry, lives and works peacefully in Cambridge, MA, United States of America, manage the sales of her products in her online Design Store which brands her name, travels world wide participating in Design Exhibitions and Lecturing.
